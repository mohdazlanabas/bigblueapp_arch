Queue Management & IoT Parking

Fair driver assignment with physical queue tracking

Smart Queue System

  • Geo-Fenced Check-In: GPS + QR sign-in at airport/mall zones
  • Queue Categories: Budget Taxi, Premier Taxi, Limo, MPV
  • Real-Time Visibility: Position, estimated time, cars ahead/behind

  • Fairness: Algorithm ensures equitable job distribution

  • Sensors: Ultrasonic/LiDAR detecting car presence

  • Violation Alerts: Wrong bay, no show, early exit
  • Admin Dashboard: Real-time occupancy, driver ID per bay
